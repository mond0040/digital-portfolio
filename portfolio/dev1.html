Original Web Page

Comm2po is a digital agency and this is a place where I got my first professional work. I worked for a graphic design internship and also as a web designer. This is one of my projects to redesign service pages on the company website. There are 7 pages under services (web design, social media management, “SEO, SEM & SMO”, content marketing, tailored marketing, branding, and media placement). I liked the idea of giving color to represent each service but the design was very flat with one image and paragraphs that were not user-friendly. So here’s what I did. I kept the color and added a tab function designs and organized paragraph with 4 categories (what we do, objectives, complementary services and normal development time). This will help users to find the information they are looking for easily.
