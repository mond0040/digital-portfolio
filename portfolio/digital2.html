Marketing Motion Graphic Video

Cansell is a Japanese company providing a platform where people can sell hotels that they need to cancel. Because of that, they don’t have to pay cancelation fees and also hotels don’t have to find a new customer to fill up space. I liked the idea of this service and I decided to make their infographic video for motion graphic class.
