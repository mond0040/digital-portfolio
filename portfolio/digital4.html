Inforgraphic Video

The infographic animation about Canada was created by our team for a project management class at Algonquin College. This video explains 5 interesting facts about Canada. It is not just showing what Canada has, but how interesting it is to visit Canada. We choose to work on infographic animation because it has the power to engage with people online, deliver information and tell stories well. Now, soundless viewing on social media is growing efficiently. Because of that, motion graphics would be more powerful than films to get more views. My role was to create graphics on illustrator and animate them on aftereffects.
