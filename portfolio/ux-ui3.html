Mobile Application Design

We developed interactive interfaces and content for the gas station campaign for a client and we implemented the policy and branding guide from Canadian Tire Gas+ which is a potential company for the client. We designed the interface to be an effective user-centered campaign that engages the target audience of Generation Z. The purpose of this project was to increase sales in the Gas+ c-stores as well as to promote sales for the Canadian Tire through promotions.
